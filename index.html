What we do

At Coco Experiments, we create simple, effective solutions to real-world problems. We believe in the power of thoughtful design and purposeful technology.

Our approach is straightforward: identify meaningful problems, design elegant solutions, and build with care and precision.

Our Philosophy

We're guided by a few core principles:

Simplicity. The best solutions are often the simplest ones. We strip away complexity and focus on what truly matters.

Purpose. We only build things that serve a genuine need and improve daily life in tangible ways.

Quality. We care deeply about craftsmanship and attention to detail in everything we create.

Open Source

We believe in the power of open source software to drive innovation and collaboration. We regularly contribute to the community with tools and libraries that solve everyday problems.

Let's Build Together

Beyond our own products, we selectively take on client projects that align with our values. We're particularly interested in collaborating on initiatives that:

• Prioritize user experience over unnecessary features
• Have the potential to make a positive impact
